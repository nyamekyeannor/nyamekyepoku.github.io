Meet The Chef

Let's talk about my passion for food! Well, let me clarify by saying "Good food" made with love. Growing up in a big household my parents had to prepared new, fresh meals almost every week. We would often invite other families and neighbors over for dinner and good music. Seeing the joy they had serving people and how it brought many together stirred in me the same passion. Fast forward years down the line Chay's scratch Kitchen was birthed, where I cater meal preps but really enjoy hosting people to experience extraordinary food and music. Chay's Scratch Kitchen has catered to birthday parties, church picnic, and many more. Currently, because of my wife and I's big move to Texas Chays Scratch Kitchen has taken a hiatus and primarily post most home creations on Instagram in order to inspire many.
